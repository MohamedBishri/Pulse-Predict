# PulsePredict — 3-Minute Winning Pitch

## 1. Hook (20 seconds)
Heart disease often develops silently. Many people see individual numbers—blood pressure, cholesterol, glucose—but do not understand how those indicators combine into risk. PulsePredict turns scattered health data into one explainable early-warning experience in under a minute.

## 2. Problem (25 seconds)
Current risk tools are often difficult to understand, disconnected from follow-up, or produce a score without explaining why. This reduces trust and makes it harder for users to take a meaningful next step.

## 3. Solution (35 seconds)
PulsePredict is an AI-powered preventive-health platform. A user creates a secure account, enters nine common indicators, and receives:
- a risk probability and Low / Moderate / High category,
- the strongest contributing factors,
- a clear next-step recommendation,
- a personal history for tracking change over time.

## 4. Live Demo (60 seconds)
1. Sign in.
2. Click **Use demo data**.
3. Calculate risk.
4. Explain the circular risk score and top factors.
5. Open **Model Lab** to show algorithm comparison and transparency.
6. Print the result report.
7. Open Admin to show platform-level impact metrics.

## 5. Innovation (20 seconds)
The innovation is not prediction alone. PulsePredict combines prediction, explainability, behavior-oriented recommendations, longitudinal tracking, bilingual access, and an architecture ready for clinical datasets and health-system integration.

## 6. Impact and Scalability (25 seconds)
The platform can support awareness campaigns, employee wellness programs, primary-care pre-screening, insurance prevention programs, and remote health initiatives. The same architecture can be extended to diabetes, stroke, and chronic kidney disease.

## 7. Closing (15 seconds)
PulsePredict changes a health score from a frightening number into an understandable action plan. Earlier awareness creates earlier conversations—and earlier conversations can improve outcomes.
